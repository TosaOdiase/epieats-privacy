1. Introduction

Welcome to EpiEats. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Allergen Preferences: You can select your food allergens (dairy, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, gluten, soy, sesame). This information is stored locally on your device only.
  • Menu Photos: Photos you take or select from your photo library to scan restaurant menus. These images are processed to detect allergens but are not permanently stored on our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we access your device's location to show nearby restaurants. Location data is used in real-time and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Camera Access: We request camera permission only when you choose to scan a menu. Camera access is used solely for capturing menu photos.
  • Photo Library Access: We request photo library access only when you choose to upload an existing photo from your device.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identifiable information
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not track your browsing or app usage with analytics
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Store your allergen preferences locally on your device to personalize your experience
  • Analyze menu photos to detect potential allergens using AI technology
  • Display nearby restaurants based on your current location (when permission is granted)
  • Provide restaurant information including menus, logos, and location details

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your allergen preferences and app settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on our servers or in any cloud database.
  • Temporary Processing: Menu photos you scan are temporarily processed by third-party AI services (see Section 5) but are not permanently stored.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Gemini API

  • Purpose: Analyzing menu photos to detect ingredients and potential allergens
  • Data Shared: Menu photos only (no personal information)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2 Google Maps API

  • Purpose: Displaying restaurant locations and nearby restaurants
  • Data Shared: Your location (only when you grant permission)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.3 Logo.dev API

  • Purpose: Fetching restaurant brand logos
  • Data Shared: Restaurant names only

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Modify: You can view and change your allergen preferences at any time within the app
  • Delete Data: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling removes all app-related information
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke camera, photo library, and location permissions at any time through your device's Settings app
  • Opt-Out of Location: You can decline or disable location access without affecting core app functionality

7. Children's Privacy

EpiEats does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Our app does not have age restrictions in the App Store, but we do not target children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Accuracy of Information

Important Notice: Allergen information displayed in EpiEats is generated using artificial intelligence and may not be 100% accurate or complete. Menu items, ingredients, and restaurant information may change without notice. Always verify allergen information directly with the restaurant staff and consult your healthcare provider for medical advice regarding food allergies.

9. Data Retention

  • Your allergen preferences are stored on your device until you change them or uninstall the app
  • Menu photos are processed in real-time and are not retained by us
  • Restaurant data is cached locally for performance but is not linked to your personal information

10. International Users

EpiEats is designed for users in the United States. If you use our app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be processed in the United States where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the categories of personal information we collect and how we use it. Since we do not collect or sell personal information, these rights are not applicable to EpiEats users.

13. Data Breach Notification

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your information is minimal. However, if we become aware of any security incident that may affect your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
