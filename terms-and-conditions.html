2. Description of Service

EpiEats is a mobile application that provides informational assistance to users with food allergies by:

  • Allowing users to scan restaurant menus using their device camera
  • Analyzing menu items for potential allergens based on FDA's 9 major food allergens
  • Flagging menu items that may contain allergens from the user's profile
  • Providing citations to FDA regulations (FALCPA 2004, FASTER Act 2021)
  • Storing user allergen profiles and restaurant data locally on the device

FDA Allergen Framework: EpiEats bases its allergen analysis on the FDA's 9 major food allergens as defined by the Food Allergen Labeling and Consumer Protection Act (FALCPA) of 2004 and the FASTER Act of 2021:

  1. Milk (Dairy)
  2. Eggs
  3. Fish
  4. Shellfish (Crustacean)
  5. Tree Nuts
  6. Peanuts
  7. Wheat (Gluten)
  8. Soybeans (Soy)
  9. Sesame
